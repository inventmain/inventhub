Why We Started InventHub: Confronting the Innovator's Dilemma

Patents should be the fruits and engines of innovation, but the reality is often grim. We've seen too many innovators facing the same challenges:

  • Numerous idle patents becoming ongoing cost centers
  • Low return on investment, discouraging further innovation
  • Weak bargaining power with single patents, failing to realize commercial value
  • Prohibitively high costs of independent enforcement actions

InventHub was created to break this cycle. We believe that by assembling scattered innovative strengths into a collaborative network, we can systematically solve these pain points and restore innovation to its rightful value.
